OUR VISION

There are many projects out there, sadly many of never reach their full potential, partly due to lack of technical skill and partly due to leadership ability, it is very hard work starting a community from new. Where we plan to be different is that we've done this before in other games on our own and it has worked, this is the first time all of us are teaming up for one project.

We are a group of friends who have known each other for years and role-played together on different servers/game modes, We all went through the joys of growing up, finishing school, getting jobs and role-play communities have been a thing that has been a huge benefit to us.

"we are not here to mess about for a couple of months and then vanish, we want this community to last."

We are currently in the alpha phase of development and many features are being added, removed, tweaked and thought out. What you see is what you get at this point, but do know that our team is fully dedicated to the community and all of us want to build something worthwhile.
